Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 12, 2026

Calm Progress is developed by Venkata Mahalingam. This policy describes how the app handles your information.

Data collection

Calm Progress does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app does not require an account, does not use analytics or tracking, and makes no network requests of any kind.

Local preferences

The app saves a small number of preferences on your device so your settings are preserved between sessions:

  • Your selected meditation style
  • Your chosen session duration
  • Whether the countdown timer is visible

These preferences are stored locally on your device using the operating system's standard settings storage. They are never transmitted off your device and are not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-party services

Calm Progress does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising frameworks.

Tracking

Calm Progress does not track you as defined by Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. The app does not access the device advertising identifier (IDFA) and does not share data with data brokers or advertising networks.

Data retention & deletion

Because Calm Progress does not collect any personal data, there is no data to retain or delete. The local preferences stored on your device can be removed at any time by deleting the app.

Children's privacy

The app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected, the app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

Your rights under GDPR (European users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation. Because Calm Progress does not collect, process, or store any personal data, these rights are not applicable in practice. No personal data is transmitted to any server.

Your rights under CCPA (California users)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act grants you specific rights regarding your personal information. Because Calm Progress does not collect or sell any personal information, there is no data subject to these rights.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach me at venkat@venkat.io.
